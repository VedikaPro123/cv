VERSUS TAEKWONDO CLASSES


We have pledged to build India as a Sporting Nation. With this as our vision, we have made ourselves fully equipped to providing with all the essential tools for Sportsmanship. We hold our instructors, courses, and student support to the highest standard so you receive the best training experience possible.Our Classes are divided by age and skill to provide the best possible match of teaching style to each student’s needs. All children are taught at their own pace to develop their present capabilities and bring out their maximum potential.

Our aim at Versus Taekwondo academy is to provide proper guidance and excellent opportunities to lead a healthy lifestyle. We provide a challenging environment to develop players’ mental and physical abilities. We at Versus Taekwondo Academy provide various taekwondo and sports activities which uplift youth participation and in turn helps them to build a positive attitude. Our coaching programs are established by experts who are highly committed and take taekwondo to new levels of enjoyment, dedication, and professionalism.

MISSION


Develop and grow Taekwondo throughout the world, from a grass roots level all the way through to an elite level, to provide all with the opportunity to play, watch and enjoy the sport regardless of age, gender, religion, ethnicity or ability.

VISION


Our Vision is to be one of the top most sports academies in India, committed to the overall development of its members and use the teaching of taekwondo as a vehicle to help improve the lives of our students improve their physical health and mental resilience.

CORE VALUES


Honesty, Deep Respect for each Individual, Give Beyond Commitment,
Quality above all, Bring smiles through Creation.
